Marriage Indicator


Generally, the age at first marriage for females has been increasing over the past decades globally. However, in some regions, growth has stalled or has not been enduring. East Asia has oscillated between ages in the mid-twenties, peaking at age 26 in 1996, while Latin America has experienced virtually no growth and remains at age 23.
